
# Pizza Order Form

I have created a form which allows you to order a pizza online.


## Instructions

Select the size of your pizza.

Select your drinks.

Select your payment method.

Click the "Submit Order" button

### Pizza Sizes

Small (6 slices) - $6.75

Medium (8 slices) - $7.75

Large (12 slices) - $10.50

### Toppings

Artichoke - $1.50

Bacon Strips - $1.25

Broccoli - $1.50

Extra Cheese - $1.00

Red Onions - $1.25

### Drinks

12 oz. Can - $1.50

16 oz. Fountain - $1.75

10 oz. Fountain - $1.25

One Liter - $2.25

### Payment Method

Credit Card

pp (Pay Pal)

ve (Venmo)

ca (Cash)

### Submitting Your Order
Once you have completed all of the steps above, click the "Submit Order" button.
